The Layered Rules

Recomp on top, blood pressure underneath

Hit the macros up top — and the food choices below make sure the BP needle moves in the right direction at the same time.

1.

Sodium ≤ 1,500 mg/day

DASH-strict. Hidden salt lives in bread, cheese, sauces & restaurant meals. Rinse every can.

2.

Potassium ≥ 4,000 mg/day

The single strongest dietary BP lever. Potato w/ skin, sweet potato, banana, beans, leafy greens, yogurt.

3.

Magnesium ≥ 400 mg/day

Vascular relaxation. Almonds, pumpkin seeds, spinach, oats, 85% dark chocolate.

4.

Nitrate hit, daily

Beetroot OR 2× servings of rocket / spinach / beet greens. NO pathway → arterial dilation.

5.

Omega-3 ≥ 2 g EPA+DHA/day

Fatty fish 3–4×/week. Sardines & salmon do the heavy lifting.

6.

Saturated fat ≤ 15 g/day

The endothelial trade-off. Steak limited to 1×/week, no aged cheeses, butter on the bench.

7.

Fibre ≥ 35 g/day

Gut microbiome → SCFAs → BP. Lentils, oats, berries, leafy greens stack into this naturally.

8.

No aged cheeses

Blue, parmesan, feta, aged gouda — high tyramine + high sodium. Fresh cheese only (cottage, ricotta, fresh mozzarella).

9.

Caffeine ≤ 2 cups, before 12:00

Doesn't push BP long-term but the afternoon dose disrupts sleep, which does. Sleep is a BP lever too.

10.

Hydrate to 3 L/day

+500–750 ml on run days. No salt in water (hypertension). Coconut water for post-run electrolytes.

11.

Coconut aminos, not soy sauce

90% less sodium for the same umami. One swap, easy win.

12.

Last food 1.5+ h before bed

Bedtime 23:00 → kitchen closes 21:30. Pre-bed casein still allowed (it's planned).

The Cook

Two cook sessions a week

Sunday batches 45 minutes of mostly hands-off work into 5 weeknight dinners and 4 lunches. Tuesday is a 30-minute top-up for the bolognese.

Sunday · 18:00 – 18:45

  1. Roast 800 g chicken breast @ 200°C, 22 min (paprika + garlic + pepper, no salt). Cool, slice, fridge → 4 lunches.
  2. While chicken roasts: boil 6 baby potatoes skin-on (15 min) · cook 120 g dry quinoa (12 min) · roast bell peppers + courgette + 600 g sweet potato cubes (200°C alongside chicken).
  3. Grate 2 raw beetroots into a sealed container → keeps 5 days.
  4. Hard-boil 6 eggs (10 min while above runs).
  5. Cook 200 g dry green lentils (20 min) if doing L4 this week.

Tuesday · 18:30 – 19:00

  1. Turkey or lean beef bolognese — 500 g mince + no-salt passata + onion + garlic + oregano. 25 min total.
  2. → Feeds L2 Wednesday + Thursday lunch.
  3. Doesn't batch past 3 days well (quality drops). Tuesday is the only active mid-week cook.

Fresh daily: eggs, salmon, sardines (open same day), white fish (10 min bake), yogurt / cottage cheese / Skyr from the fridge.

The Doha Watch

Brand-specific notes

Cottage cheese

Almarai 2% is everywhere — but it runs ~400 mg Na / 100 g. Hunt for an imported low-sodium variant at Carrefour or LuLu. If Almarai is the only option, halve the pre-bed portion and add water + lemon to dilute.

Greek yoghurt

Dodoni 0% is your default — already in the rotation. Avoid Dodoni 8% — sat fat climbs fast.

Bread

Doha bakery sourdough is salt-loaded by default. The Sultan Center bake-in-store rye has been the lowest I've found locally — check the label on the day.

Stock cubes

Skip them entirely. One cube wrecks the sodium budget for the meal. Flavour with lemon, herbs, garlic, paprika and cumin instead.

Out & About

Hydration, fuelling, eating out

💧 Hydration

  • 3 L plain water baseline every day.
  • +500 ml on run days (≈3.5 L total) to cover sweat losses.
  • 300–500 ml coconut water post-run — natural electrolytes, ~25 mg Na, no added sugar.
  • Coffee / tea count 50% toward the total. Caffeine is mildly diuretic.
  • No salt in water. Hypertension contra-indication.

🏃 Training-day fuel timing

  • Run days: S1 (whey + banana) 30–60 min before · 250 ml coconut water during if >45 min · D1 or D3 within 60 min post.
  • Cardio days: Same as runs but a half-banana before is plenty.
  • Rest days: Skip the pre-workout S1 — use S2, S4 or S5 instead.
  • Gym days (unplanned): Banana 30 min before, whey shake within 60 min post.

🍽️ Eating out (Doha)

  • Grilled fish, chicken, or steak as the protein. One portion only.
  • Ask for "no salt, no sauce, sauce on side" — most kitchens will accommodate.
  • Double the green vegetable side; skip the bread basket.
  • Pick one carb — not rice + naan + bread.
  • Water with lemon. No soft drinks, no cocktails.
  • Skip the cheese course entirely.
  • Pizza only at the Saturday free-meal slot. ≤4 slices, share if possible.

📅 Review & rotation

  • Week 4 (mid-Jun): bored? swap a protein (mackerel for sardines, sirloin once for chicken). Keep the structure.
  • Week 8 (mid-Jul): re-pull Garmin TDEE. If fat-loss on track, hold. If stalled, cut a snack before changing macros.
  • Week 12 (mid-Aug): reassess BP trend. If still high, layer in 140 ml unsweetened beetroot juice AM.
  • Logging below 5/7 days: simplify — same B + L + D every weekday until compliance returns. Variety is the failure mode.
